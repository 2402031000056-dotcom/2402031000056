Our Story

Scoop Dreams began in 2015 in a small garage with just one churn. Our mission has always been simple: bring a smile to people's faces, one scoop at a time.

We partner with local dairy farms to get the freshest milk and cream. Our fruits are sourced from regional farmers' markets, ensuring that every bite tastes like summer.

We are committed to sustainability, using biodegradable cups and spoons.
